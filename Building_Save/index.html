Save The Trees

  • High Concept: This game is about trying to take the buildings out and put the trees in.
  • Theme: The theme used for this game is preservationist.
  • Mandated Variety: The input is the keyboard, there is some randomness to this in terms of how the ball moves. The player is trying to saves the tress.
  • Player Experience Goals: The player should be able to press the keys fast on the keyboard and be able to move fast, so they can try to save the ball from falling.
  • Gameplay: The player can move around the map using the arrow keys on the keyboard. They can start using the spacebar. The goal is to take all of the buildings out.
  • Prototype Goal: The prototype is evaluating moving with the keys and being able to detect who has won depending on the gameplay. Also keep track of the lives used.
  • Features: The player has control of a character and is able to move just left or right. Also the way the ball moves is unique because it is at random. Also it has cool sounds when the ball hits the building.
  • Setting/Premise: The setting takes place in the future when the Earth is filled with CO2. The people are trying to bring back a healthy lifestyle.
  • Story: People don't want to live in a world that is fulled with smog and CO2.
  • Target Audience: People who are board and just want to play a quick game.
  • Play Time: The game take on an average around 5 minute to play.
  • Strategies: Player should release the ball at the side, so that it can travel up and get more points.
