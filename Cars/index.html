Too Many Cars

  • High Concept: This game is about the player avoiding the other cars.
  • Theme: The theme used for this game is cars.
  • Mandated Variety: The input is the keyboard and there is some randomness to this in terms of how the cars move.
  • Player Experience Goals: The player should be able to press the keys on the keyboard, so that they can avoid the cars.
  • Gameplay: The Player has to move around the map and avoid the cars. The player has 3 lives to get the highest score.
  • Prototype Goal: The prototype is game angles.
  • Features: The player has control of how they move on the map.
  • Setting/Premise: The setting takes place on a dirt track.
  • Story: The player gets points if they avoid the cars.
  • Target Audience: People who are board and just want to play a quick game.
  • Play Time: The game take on an average around 5 minute to play.
  • Strategies: If the player moves to the corners of the map at the start of the game then they have a better chance of avoiding the cars.
