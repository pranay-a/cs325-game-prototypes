  • High Concept: This game is about trying to match all the cards in a certain amount of time.
  • Theme: The theme used for this game is person who will do whatever it takes.
  • Mandated Variety: The input is the mouse, there is no randomness to this game. This is mostly about memorization and how fast a player can think. The play style is achiever you are trying to achieve an objective.
  • Player Experience Goals: The player should be able to click on the mouse fast and be able to move fast, so that they flip all the cards in a certain amount of time.
  • Gameplay: The player can click using the mouse and they have to try to match all of the cards together. The player has to try to get the score less than 300 to win the game. This game is purely based on how fast a player can click and memorize all of the cards. The player has to match all of cards based on the number or the type. If this was not the prototype there would have been obstacles to distract the player and this will make it harder for the player to win the game.
  • Prototype Goal: The prototype is evaluating moving with the mouse and being able to detect where the mouse was clicked.
  • Features: The player has control of a character and is able to move using the mouse. The mouse can click on the cards to turn them over.
  • Setting/Premise: The setting takes place in downtown Las Vegas. The good guy is the player and he is a spy on a mission and he has to win a lot of money playing this game, so they he can show his fake boss he is capable of doing a different mission. He has to win it fast because there are other players willing to steal the money.
  • Story: On the mission, The player looses all the betting money and the fake boss is angry and is willing to kill the player for anything. In order to save his life, the player is willing to do anything to get the money back, so he plays the this game to win and get the money back. And if the player wins then he is saved.
  • Target Audience: People who are board and just want to play a quick game.
