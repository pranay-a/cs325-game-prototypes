Dark

  • High Concept: This game is about trying get to the invisible man.
  • Theme: The theme used for this game is glow in the dark.
  • Mandated Variety: The input is the keyboard and the mouse, there is some randomness to this in terms of how the man moves.
  • Player Experience Goals: The player should be able to press the keys on the keyboard and be able to move the mouse, so that they get the man.
  • Gameplay: The Player has to get to the man when he is visible. Then the player gets points if they collide with the man when he is visible.
  • Prototype Goal: The prototype is game states, there are a lot of game states in this game.
  • Features: The player has control of the way they move.
  • Setting/Premise: The setting takes place in a city.
  • Story: When the score is more than 500 then the player wins.
  • Target Audience: People who are board and just want to play a quick game.
  • Play Time: The game take on an average around 5 minute to play.
  • Strategies: The player has to move fast to get to the man.
